Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about using Carvo

How do I add a new vehicle to my fleet?

To add a new vehicle, navigate to the Vehicles page and click the "Add Vehicle" button. Fill in the required information including make, model, license plate, and daily rate. You can also upload a photo of the vehicle for easy identification.

How can I track vehicle locations in real-time?

Vehicle locations are displayed on the interactive map on the main dashboard. Each vehicle is represented by a car icon that shows its current status (available, in use, or maintenance). Click on any vehicle marker to see detailed information.

How do I create and manage rental orders?

Go to the Orders page and click "New Order". Select a customer and vehicle, set the rental dates, and specify the daily rate. The system will automatically calculate the total amount. You can track order status and manage payments from the same page.

Can I export reports and data?

Yes! On the Reports page, you can generate various reports including revenue analysis, vehicle utilization, and customer insights. Use the "Export Report" button to download reports in PDF or Excel format for further analysis.

How do I set up notifications?

Navigate to Settings > Notifications to configure your notification preferences. You can choose to receive alerts for new orders, vehicle maintenance, payment reminders, and system updates. Notifications can be sent via email or in-app.

What payment methods are supported?

Carvo supports multiple payment methods including credit cards, bank transfers, and digital wallets. You can configure payment settings in the Settings > General section. All transactions are securely processed and encrypted.
